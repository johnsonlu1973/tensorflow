Research Bits: May 26
研究亮點：5 月 26 日
Simultaneous energy generation and emission Researchers from the Institute of Science Tokyo and University of Osaka designed an organic semiconductor device that can both generate electricity from light and emit bright visible light. The researchers used two multiple-resonance thermally activated delayed fluorescence (MR-TADF) molecules, v-DABNA and QAO, in a simple layered structure. Their device achieved 1.36% power-conversion efficiency and 2% light-emission efficiency simultaneously. It emitted bright red light with a luminance of 1,000 cd/m2, matching the brightness of commercial smartphone displays.
