I replaced my NAS with Google Drive for a month and barely noticed
我用Google Drive取代了NAS一個月，幾乎沒有察覺到差異
I didn’t intend to do this experiment, but my month-long trip away from home forced me to shut down my NAS for a good few weeks. What I expected to be a temporary inconvenience ended up feeling… normal. The NAS is such an ingrained part of my local file access workflow that I assumed I’d miss it immediately, but with a portable SSD in my hand and Google Drive practically moving everywhere with me, I had little reason to think about my home setup at all. For the most part, everything just worked, although there were still a couple of moments where a local network storage solution would have been more suitable.
一次意外的NAS停機實驗讓我發現，Google Drive作為雲端儲存方案竟然能無縫取代我的本地NAS系統。這段經歷挑戰了我對NAS不可或缺的既有認知。
