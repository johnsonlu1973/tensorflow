The emergence of the web data infrastructure layer for AI
AI 網絡數據基礎設施層的興起
AI is booming. New use cases are emerging each day. To capitalize on the technology’s potential, enterprises require data at scale. In many cases, though, the relevant information is blocked or unstructured, which limits its use by AI models.&nbsp; To understand this challenge, consider the foundation of the web itself. The web was not designed for the automated discovery and retrieval that new AI applications demand. Overcoming this inherent design constraint requires infrastructure. The next frontier in AI may depend on a new web data infrastructure layer that can enable models to discover and map this ever-expanding digital realm. This layer must be able to navigate hundreds of millions of existing web domains and billions of new URLs created each week, delivering real-time information
AI 快速發展，新應用日益涌現，企業需要大規模數據來充分利用其潛力，但許多相關資訊被限制或結構不當，限制了 AI 模型的使用。
